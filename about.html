about

THIEF, THE FIRST SOLO VENTURE FROM JOHN MCNULTY (SWINE, COCKTAIL KINGDOM HOSPITALITY GROUP), HAS A MISSION TO STEAL BACK THE TIME SPENT AWAY FROM YOUR FAVORITE BAR. A MODERN TAKE ON THE NEIGHBORHOOD BAR, THIEF IS INSPIRED IN DESIGN AND ENERGY BY THE 1980'S NYC GRAFFITI, ART, AND MUSIC SCENE. THE MENUS FEATURE RIFFS ON CLASSIC COCKTAILS, AN EXTENSIVE WINE PROGRAM, AND ELEVATED BAR FOOD. THE SPACE THAT CHANNELS THE EDGINESS OF 80’S NYC. DESIGNED BY JASON VOLENEC OF VOLENEC STUDIO (BODEGA NEGRA, MISS LILY’S, BUTTERFLY), THE 50-SEAT SPACE INCORPORATES RAW, NOSTALGIC ELEMENTS MEANT TO EVOKE THE BYGONE ERA - THINK A BLACK AND SILVER CHECKERBOARD CEILING AND ORIGINAL FLYERS FROM PUNK AND HIP-HOP SHOWS AND A BEAUTIFUL PIECE BY THE FAMED ARTIST OF THAT ERA, MARIPOL, FEATURING POLAROIDS OF BASQUIAT IN HIS DOWNTOWN NYC LOFT IN 1982.

Our Mission

AT THIEF NYC, OUR MISSION IS TO SAFEGUARD AND CELEBRATE THE ENDURING ALLURE OF NEW YORK CITY, BLENDING ITS TIMELESS GRIT AND CHARM WITH CONTEMPORARY RELEVANCE. WE ARE DEDICATED TO SHARING THIS CAPTIVATING ESSENCE WITH GUESTS NATIONWIDE, ENCAPSULATING OUR FAVORITE ELEMENTS THAT MAKE THE CITY SO IRRESISTIBLY ENIGMATIC. FROM THE VIBRANT STREET ART CULTURE TO THE PULSATING UNDERGROUND MUSIC SCENES, WE ASPIRE TO HARMONIOUSLY AMALGAMATE THE CULTURAL MOVEMENTS THAT HAVE DEFINED THIS CITY. OUR GOAL IS TO TRANSFORM THE URBAN EXPERIENCE INTO A VIBRANT, SEVEN-DAY-A-WEEK, LIVING ENTITY WITHIN THE CONFINES OF OUR FOUR WALLS. MUCH LIKE THE SEISMIC CULTURAL SHIFTS SPARKED BY NYC'S ART AND CREATIVITY, WE ENVISION THIEF NYC AS MORE THAN JUST A PROVIDER OF CRIMINALLY GOOD FOOD AND DRINKS. WE BELIEVE IT IS A CATALYST FOR INSPIRATION, TRANSCENDING GEOGRAPHICAL BOUNDARIES, AND LEAVING AN INDELIBLE MARK ON THE HEARTS AND MINDS OF ALL WHO ENCOUNTER IT.

Our Vision

AT THIEF NYC, OUR VISION IS TO BE THE MODERN-DAY ARCHITECTS OF URBAN TRANSFORMATION, DRAWING INSPIRATION FROM OUR WILLIAMSBURG FLAGSHIP LOCATION AND TAKE THE CHANCE TO EMBRACE THE OPPORTUNITY TO CULTIVATE A MULTINATIONAL BRAND THAT IDENTIFIES UNMET NEEDS AND FILLS THEM WITH INNOVATION. THIEF NYC IS NOT JUST A HAVEN FOR CITY-DWELLERS; IT'S A BEACON FOR MAJOR METROPOLISES SEEKING INSPIRATION FROM THE VIBRANT HEART OF NEW YORK.


WE POSSESS AN UNQUENCHABLE THIRST TO BOTH PRESERVE AND PROVIDE THE GRITTY AND CHARMING ASPECTS OF NEW YORK, PAST AND PRESENT. THINK OF US AS TODAY'S BENEVOLENT BANDITS, DEVOID OF CRIMINAL INTENT. WE COLLECT THE FRAGMENTS OF URBAN LIFE, CURATE THEM WITH CARE, AND CULTIVATE THE ESSENCE OF WHAT MAKES THE CONCRETE JUNGLE PULSE. AS THE MODERN-DAY ROBIN HOOD OF CITIES, WE SPREAD THE WEALTH OF INSPIRATION, INNOVATION, AND CULTURAL RICHNESS, ENSURING THAT THIEF NYC IS THE CONDUIT FOR URBAN EVOLUTION, A TREASURE TROVE OF IDEAS, AND A BEACON OF PROGRESS FOR CITIES WORLDWIDE.
